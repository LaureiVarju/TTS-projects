How to Cook Mom’s Mac and Cheese

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound Macoroni
  • 1 pound Gouda Cheese
  • 1 stick Unsalted Butter
  • 1 cup Oat Milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
    Directions
  • 1) Cook Mac in 6 quarts boiling water for 12 mins.
  • 2) Cut Gouda into small cubes approx 1/4 in.
  • 3) Bring oat milk to simmer on med high and then reduce heat to low
  • 4) Cut butter into 6 cubes and leave at room temp.
  • 5) Remove Mac from heat and drain completely then place in large bowl.
  • 6) Cut butter into 6 cubes and leave at room temp.
  • 7) Add gouda to Mac and stir until cheese begins to melt.
  • 8) Add milk to Mac.
  • 9) Stir until cheese completely melted.
  • 10) Add butter at the end and stir in.
  • 11) Plate and serve.
